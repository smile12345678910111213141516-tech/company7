Our story

About Repowering Partners

We are a principal owner-operator focused on acquiring, upgrading, and operating solar farms for long-term profit.

Repowering Partners was built around a simple conviction: long-term value comes from owning real assets. We buy solar farms for our own portfolio, invest in upgrades, and operate them with strict performance discipline.

We are not a brokerage and we do not buy assets on behalf of third parties as a service. Our model is principal investing: acquire carefully, improve intelligently, and hold for durable operating returns.

We focus on projects with solid fundamentals that can be improved through technology refresh, better operations, and rigorous asset management.

What we stand for

Principles behind every acquisition and upgrade decision.

  • Principal ownership We invest our own capital and run what we buy.
  • Profit with discipline Every project is evaluated on long-term cash flow and asset value growth.
  • Upgrade over replacement Targeted repowering and optimization can unlock major performance gains.
  • Transparent operations Clear reporting and measurable performance are core to our model.
